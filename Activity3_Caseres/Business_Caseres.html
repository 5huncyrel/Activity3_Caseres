About El Zona Guitara


Tradition since 2015

El Zona Guitara was founded in Cagayan, 15th Musika St, Misamis Oriental by Ms. Shun Cyrel Caseres. We wanted to create a new type of shop that could cater for acoustic guitar players of all levels.
El Zona Guitara aims to provide the best acoustic guitars that's still affordable for everyone who wants to purchase. We are always looking for the best products that meet the discerning taste of today's acoustic players. We've come a long way since our early beginnings. We always vowed that customer service would be our top priority.

Our shop is open 5 days a week for you to visit and try the guitars before you buy. If you can't visit our shop, You can order on our website for fast transaction.

Experience our acoustic guitar only here in "El Zona Guitara".
